## Research Themes & Focus Areas

### Primary Research Domains
1. **Open Government Data & Linked Data**
   - Statistical data integration and interoperability
   - Linked Open Data analytics
   - Data quality assessment

2. **Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning**
   - Large Language Models in government contexts
   - Graph Neural Networks
   - Deep Learning for time series and sensor data
   - Explainable AI (XAI)

3. **E-Governance & Democratic Participation**
   - Electronic participation systems
   - Citizen engagement platforms
   - Democratic deliberation technologies
   - Government service innovation

4. **Big Data Analytics**
   - High-performance computing applications
   - Data intelligence systems
   - Predictive analytics for public services

### Research Impact
- Development of government AI policies and frameworks
- Creation of open data analytics tools
- Enhancement of citizen engagement mechanisms
- Training and education programs for open data literacy
